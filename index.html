Let me show you how to play:

  1. You have to select a number from the given choices.
  2. The dice will roll after every 10 seconds.
  3. If your selected number matches with the number on dice, you will get 10 points.
  4. And if not you can try again.
  5. You can change your choice as many times you want before the dice rolls.
